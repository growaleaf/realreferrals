Straight answers

  • How much will I earn?Unknown. Possibly nothing — the terms say plainly that most affiliates earn nothing, and that is true here too. The difference is the other side of it: every sale that does happen pays, on schedule, and you can watch it mature in a ledger nobody edits for a screenshot. Nobody here will show you a Ferrari.
  • Is this MLM?No. There is nothing to buy, no starter pack, no downline. Recruiting other promoters pays you $0. You earn only when a real customer buys a real product through your link.
  • Why is the deal so small?Because small is what real looks like. A $4.99 pack clears checkout in about a minute. The $50M allocation has a nine-step procedure because the procedure is the product — it exists to keep you in the chain, paying fees. We'd rather pay you $2.28 that exists than promise you a million that doesn't.
  • Which countries?Anywhere PayPal can receive money. We don't keep a country list — open your PayPal and check it can receive funds where you are. If it can't, this desk can't pay you, and we'd rather tell you that now than after you've shared a hundred links.
  • Who actually buys these packs?Not your deal groups — your other chats. Family groups, church and mosque groups, school parents, office chats, diaspora cousins abroad. It's a party game: people buy it to play it together tonight. The Drop Kit maps which message belongs in which chat.
  • What's in it for the desk?The other $2.27. That's the whole model — we keep half of net, you get half. When you earn, we earn. When you earn nothing, we earn nothing. Incentives pointing the same direction is what a real deal looks like.
